How to make a Bologna Cake

Why you should make this monstrosity:

If you have a large group of people that you need to feed, this bologna cake will be a great option.

Balogna cake, while it may seem complex, actually only includes three ingredients.

It can be prepared In less than 20 minutes, resulting in a tasty appetizer and a conversation starter.

Bologna Cake Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cream cheese (1 Â½ packages, room temperature)
  • 3 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 package bologna
  • Cheese Whiz, for decorating

Bologna Cake Instructions

  1. Place the cream cheese in a large bowl. Stir in the onion powder using a spatula.
  2. Take one slice of bologna and pat it dry with paper towels; place it on a plate.
  3. With the use of an offset spatula, spread a layer of the cream cheese mixture on top of the bologna.
  4. Add another slice of bologna, patted dry with paper towels, and spread about 1 tablespoon of the cream cheese mixture on
    top. Repeat the process until you run out of bologna slices.
  5. Spread the remaining cream cheese mixture onto the sides and top of the bologna cake.
  6. If you would like to decorate your cake, you can cut the tip out of a piping bag or the corner of a zip-top bag, add a piping
    dip, and fill it with Cheese Whiz.
  7. Finally, pipe the Cheese Whiz on top of the cake. Then, slice and serve immediately.
