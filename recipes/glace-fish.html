How to make a Glace Fish Mold

A brief description on this fine cusine:

Wholly customisable, this glace fish mold originates from some old-timey all-american cookbook, and is sure to turn head!

While it may seem difficult, this crowd-pleaser is relatively simple to make, it will just be the mold itself that is hardest to source.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 envelope plain gelatine
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup of flaked fish (use any flaked cooked fish/shellfish)
  • 1/4 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/2 unpleeled cucumber, cut
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped pimiento
  • 1/4 cup celery, diced

Glace Fish Mold Instructions

  1. Soften gelatine in cold water, dissolve in boiling water.
  2. Add sugar, salt, lemon juice and cool.
  3. When the mixture begins to thicken, fold in fish, green pepper, cucumber wedges, onion rings, pimiento and celery
  4. Pour into an 1-quart mold, rinsed in cold water.
  5. When firm, unmold garnishing as desired.
  6. Best served with generous amounts of real mayonnaise
